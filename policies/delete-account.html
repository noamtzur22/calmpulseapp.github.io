Delete Your CalmPulse Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps within the app:

  1. Open the CalmPulse app.
  2. Tap the ⚙️ Settings icon (top-right corner).
  3. Select "My Account".
  4. Tap the button labeled "Delete My Account".

Once confirmed, your account and all linked data will be permanently deleted from our system. This action is irreversible.
